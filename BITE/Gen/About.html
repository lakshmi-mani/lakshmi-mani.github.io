About Us

BITE Magazine features creative work from youths across the globe. An exclusively online and non-profit project where every single work is executed and collated virtually, BITE Magazine is a way to inspire and be inspired. Tapping on available mediums of communication online, we hope to highlight the importance of technology and new media in day to day interaction of youths today.

FAQs

WHAT IS BITE MAGAZINE?
A group of us have always wanted to participate in a collaborative project that encompasses all forms of creativity amongst youths. The internet is not only a tool for research; in fact, it has developed into a powerful medium of communication that enables individuals to network and share multitudes of content. As such, BITE Magazine hopes to bring forth the notion that creativity transcends all boundaries (language, territorial and so on), as diverse aesthetic perspectives are brought together within a magazine that is promoted - published - online. More information can be found on our ABOUT PAGE.

IS THE MAGAZINE AVAILABLE ONLINE OR IN PRINT?
The digital issues can be viewed HERE, or you can support the magazine by purchasing your own digital and/or printed copy via MAGCLOUD.

DO YOU ACCEPT SUBMISSIONS?
We do accept a limited number of submissions in the form of art, design and fashion photography. Our submission guidelines can be found HERE.
