Tripadvisor

A Guerilla Usability Study

The App

Tripadvisor is a great app that has tons of reviews and information on restaurants and things to do around you. The app has traditionally had a focus on "pre booking", ie. planning and booking before a trip.

However, the tons of information on the website will also make it an ideal app to help find local business around your current location! I find pain points in using Tripadvisor for this purpose.

User Personas

Meet Troy! Troy is a young vibrant traveller to San Francisco who is hoping to find cool new things to do around him.

Hello Troy, you dashing fella!

Guerilla Usability Testing

I conducted my tests on 7 travellers staying in a hostel in San Francisco.

  1. Task 1: Find a highly rated comedy club in San Francisco that is open tonight that you can attend.
  2. Task 2: Find the best Italian restaurant, within a medium price range that is closest to you.
  3. Task 3: Book a hotel room within a budget of $300 for this weekend, in the SoMa neighbourhood.

I used neutral language and open ended scenarios in the tasks so as to avoid leading the users to solve them in a particular way.

Synthesis

I reviewed the videos I took, and identified what are the troubles users faced, when they had trouble, when they took a long time to figure stuff out etc.

Grouping issues by similarity, and ordering them by the users themselves.

As there were several issues that I found with the app, I decided to map it on a 2x2 of what are the issues that are important to the user vs important to the business.

A mapping of the issues important to the user vs the business

This clearly showed that trouble with search results and filtering was something that could be improved on to help both the company and the user experience.

Design Story

Next, I decided to break down this feature idea to improve search and filtering into tiny little tasks to scope out the project for myself. This also meant that I wouldn't miss out any interactions. CRUD framework was really useful in helping with that.

EPIC: Improve search and filtering

USERS CAN:

  • search for phrases
  • view suggestions when there are few search results
  • view search suggestions for no results
  • suggested phrases
  • view a search result
  • come back to search results
  • filter search results by category, budget, location
