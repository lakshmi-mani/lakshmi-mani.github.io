HelpmeHK

HelpmeHK is an app targetted at tourists travelling to Hong Kong. It gives the locations of the nearest police stations, hospitals, toilets and consulates. It gives details of the locations, such as address, directions, opening hours, and chinese name to use on taxis.

ShoppingBud

ShoppingBud is an app that lets people get the opinions of friends for things they are buying. If you need your friends' opinion, just take a picture, type in a short message, and your friends can choose yes or no to buying it.
